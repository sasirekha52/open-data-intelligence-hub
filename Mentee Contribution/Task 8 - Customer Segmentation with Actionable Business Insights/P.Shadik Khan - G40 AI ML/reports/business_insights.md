# Business Insights

## 1. High-Value Loyal Customers
These customers have high spending, frequent purchases, and recent activity.

Recommended actions:
- Provide loyalty rewards.
- Give early access to new products.
- Offer premium membership benefits.
- Avoid unnecessary discounts.

## 2. New and Promising Customers
These customers joined recently and show good engagement.

Recommended actions:
- Send welcome offers.
- Recommend popular products.
- Encourage a second purchase.
- Send personalized emails.

## 3. Discount-Driven Customers
These customers mostly purchase during promotions.

Recommended actions:
- Send limited-time discounts.
- Offer bundle deals.
- Avoid discounts outside campaign periods.
- Promote products during sales.

## 4. At-Risk Customers
These customers purchased before but have not purchased recently.

Recommended actions:
- Send re-engagement campaigns.
- Offer comeback discounts.
- Ask for customer feedback.
- Recommend newly launched products.

## 5. Low-Engagement Customers
These customers have low spending and low purchase frequency.

Recommended actions:
- Use low-cost email marketing.
- Promote entry-level products.
- Avoid expensive advertising.
- Analyze whether they should remain a priority group.
